What it builds

A structured knowledge base Claude reads automatically.

Plain markdown files organized by domain. A trigger-based loading system ensures Claude gets precisely the right context for every conversation — not everything at once, just what's relevant.

You own the files. They live on your machine. No cloud dependency, no lock-in. Works with Claude Desktop, Cursor, and any MCP-compatible client.

  • core/
    identity.md
    Your name, role, background, how you think, what you care about.
  • venture/
    my-venture.md
    What you're building, stage, metrics, positioning, competitive landscape.
  • sales/
    methodology.md
    How you sell, your process, what works, what doesn't.
  • sales/
    icp.md
    Your ideal customer profile, qualification criteria, disqualifiers.
  • communication/
    style.md
    How you write and speak. Tone, vocabulary, what you never say.
  • platform/
    linkedin.md
    Your LinkedIn voice, post structure, audience, what performs.
  • planning/
    goals.md
    Current quarter goals, milestones, what you're optimizing for.
  • network/
    contacts.jsonl
    Key people, relationship context, last interaction, notes.

Why this works

Not a system prompt. A knowledge base.

System prompts tell the AI how to act. know.help gives it something to reason from — structured facts, methodology, and context that persist across every conversation.
