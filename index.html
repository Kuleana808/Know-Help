The problem

Every time you open a new chat, you start over.

You re-explain everything Your role. Your company. The deal you're working. The person you're meeting. The way you like to communicate.
Your AI is brilliant but blind It has no memory of your context, your relationships, or your goals. Every session is the first session.

How it works

01

Build your knowledge base

Modular files organized by what matters — your background, your network, your business, your voice, your goals. You write it once.

02

Triggers load context automatically

Each file declares keywords. Mention a person's name and their full relationship history loads. Start a sales conversation and your methodology appears. No prompting required.

03

Your AI finally knows you

Every conversation starts with the right context already loaded. Less explaining. Better output. Feels like talking to someone who's been paying attention.

What lives in your knowledge base

core/ Who you are — your background, identity, skills, and team.

sales/ Methodology, buyer psychology, objection handling, ICP profiles.
platform/ Your distinct voice for each channel — LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, all separate.
planning/ Goals, pipeline, and active opportunities.
communication/ Style rules and templates broken down by channel and audience.
log/ Daily activity tracking and decision logs with full context.
