01

Claude visits know.help/setup

The setup page is written for Claude to read — not humans. It contains exact instructions: what questions to ask, what files to create, and how to structure your knowledge base. Claude follows them automatically.

STEP 1: Ask name, role, venture
STEP 2: Ask network + priorities
STEP 3: Run npx know-help init
STEP 4: Write files from answers
02

Claude interviews you

Ten questions about your role, ventures, relationships, communication style, and goals. Claude maps your answers to the right knowledge files — identity, network, ventures, voice, planning. No manual file editing required.

core/identity.md ← who you are
venture/[name].md ← what you build
network/[name].jsonl ← who matters
platform/voice.md ← how you write
planning/goals.md ← where you're going
03

Claude knows, from now on

Once set up, Claude loads your context automatically at the start of every conversation. Trigger-based loading means the right files appear at the right moment — without you asking. Your AI is permanently briefed.

// mention "TerraVault" → venture.md loads
// say "write a post" → voice.md loads
// No prompting. No re-explaining.
// Just context, automatically.

What goes in

Everything your AI needs
to stop asking.

Six domains. All built from your answers in the setup conversation. Each file has trigger keywords so the right context loads at the right moment — not everything, all at once.

core/

Who you are

Role, background, how you operate, what you care about.

"You are a CEO running a tech company from Austin..."

network/

Who matters

Every key relationship — history, context, open threads, how you work with them.

"Jordan Chen — co-founder, handling partnerships, traveling in Q1..."

venture/

What you build

Your companies, products, status, strategy, revenue, key contacts.

"TerraVault — $50M ARR, 8K customers, Series B active..."

platform/

How you write

Distinct voice for each channel. LinkedIn ≠ Twitter ≠ email. Claude knows the difference.

"LinkedIn: data-driven, no fluff. First sentence is a hook..."

sales/

How you sell

Your methodology, ICP profiles, objection handling, buyer psychology.

"Open with outcome, not feature. Enterprise buyers need..."

planning/

Where you're going

Goals, pipeline, active opportunities, this quarter's priorities.

"Q1 focus: M&A LOI by Mar 2 · hit $75K monthly profit..."

log/

What's happened

Daily activity, decisions with rationale, failures and lessons. Append-only JSONL.

"2026-02-24: Decided to cut Robyn position. Rationale: runway..."

living/

What's happening now

Living Intelligence monitors X, Reddit, LinkedIn, TikTok, RSS across your ventures — updates your files automatically.

"Pro feature — knowledge base updates itself every 6hrs"
