# Creator Voice Pack

Teach your AI to write like you — differently on every platform.

## What's included

- **platform/linkedin.md** — LinkedIn voice, content pillars, post frameworks
- **platform/twitter.md** — Twitter/X voice, thread templates, engagement tactics
- **platform/newsletter.md** — Newsletter voice, structure, and rhythm
- **communication/style.md** — Your universal writing rules and style guide

## Installation

```
know install ./creator-voice-pack
```

## How to use

After installing, customize each file with YOUR voice examples. The templates show you exactly what to fill in — paste in 3-5 real posts you've written and the AI will learn your patterns.

## Who this is for

Founders, creators, and operators who create content across multiple platforms and want their AI to match their voice on each one.
